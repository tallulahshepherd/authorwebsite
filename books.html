Do you run from your curse? Or claim it?

Fenn Kerry is back in Alderland, after fleeing seven years ago. Haunted by the mythical figure of the Fox-Fire Witch, she fears that with each step she might lose control of her unpredictable magic, and end up with the same tragic fate. Instead, she finds ghosts of another kind, in the form of the disgraced Prince Owin Maddox raising their daughter without her.

But, when Princess Angharad is threatened, only Fox-Fire will be able to save her.

She will be hunted, exiled, and betrayed before she has to make an impossible choice. Will she save her homeland and rid herself of the Fox-Fire Witch for good? Or will she ally herself with her darker half, and change the face of Alderland forever?
