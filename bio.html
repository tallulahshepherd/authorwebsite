Tallulah Carw is a British transplant living in Minnesota, where she spends her days dreaming up fantastical worlds and swoon-worthy romances. When she's not lost in her writing, you can find her cuddled up with her beloved cat, plotting their next adventure, or watching horror movies. Tallulah's love for all things spooky and mysterious shines through in her writing, transporting readers to enchanting realms filled with magic and romance.
